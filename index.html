Bangla Learning App

Are you a Bangladeshi living abroad or someone whose first language is English but has always wanted to connect more deeply with your cultural roots? Our Bangla Learning App is designed just for you!

This application is specifically crafted for English-speaking Bangladeshis who want to learn how to read Bangla. Whether you're visiting Bangladesh and want to navigate signs and menus with ease, or you're looking to engage more fully with Bangla content on social media, this app bridges the gap between you and the rich world of the Bangla language.

Why This App?

  • Connect with Culture: Understanding and reading Bangla opens up a whole new dimension of cultural connection. Engage with other Bangladeshis on a deeper level, both online and in person.
  • Ease of Travel: Visiting Bangladesh? This app will help you read signs, menus, and other essential information, making your experience more enriching and stress-free.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Social media is a powerful tool for sharing knowledge, but a lot of great content is in Bangla. With this app, you'll be able to access and participate in those conversations, expanding your horizons.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the cultural and linguistic gap between foreign-born Bangladeshis and their native counterparts. By empowering you to read Bangla, we hope to foster stronger connections within the global Bangladeshi community.
